About Us

  

Al Mahra Laundry is originated in UAE. We are the most premium laundry and dry cleaning service provider in Ras Al Khaimah. After building a good reputation in the northern emirates, we have taken a step forward to the most prestigious Dubai, setting up our state of the art laundry plant in Dubai Investment Park with more than 6000 sq ft warehouse and servicing up to 10 tons of linen every day.We have now become  ONE of the LEADING dry cleaners in Dubai catering to industries such as hospitality, airlines, retail and other industry sectors across the region. Leave your dry cleaning and laundry to us –because  we are the best!

  

Al Mahra Laundry has multiple pickup services  in Dubai including Dubai Marina, Jumeirah Beach Residences, JLT, Al Barsha, Business Bay ( Downtown Dubai)),  Al Quoz, and also  in Greens, Palm Jumeirah, Meadows, Jumeirah Village Circle, Jumeirah Triangle, Arabian Ranches, Discovery Garden, Furjan, Sports City, Motor City, AL Barari, Trade Centre, Sheikh Zayed Road, Financial Centre, Zabeel, Jumeirah Island, Barsha Heights, Jadaf, and Dubai Design District, Emirates Hills, Arjan, Al Barsha South, Um Suqeim and most of Dubai.
